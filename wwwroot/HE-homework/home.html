Customers queue for hours to be the first to try Nando's new burger

The sunset burger is Nando's newest burger. Hundreds of fans queued up in front of restraunts all across the country to be amoung the first to taste it. It is replacing the Churrasco thigh burger and while most people are thrilled with the new addition, they're sad to see it go.

Nando's, for those who don't know, is a restraunt which specializes flame-grilled peri peri chicken. It has places all over the world but originated in South Africa. There are 1094 Nando's in the world, 339 of them in the UK.

The sunset-burger is made up of :

Two flame-grilled chicken thighs, with melting cheese

Smokey red pepper chutney

All held within a soft sweet roll.
