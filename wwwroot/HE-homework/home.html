Customers queue for hours to be the first to try Nando's new burger

Nando's

The sunset burger is Nando's newest burger. Hundreds of fans queued up in front of restraunts all across the country to be amoung the first to taste it. It is replacing the Churrasco thigh burger and while most people are thrilled with the new addition, they're sad to see it go.

Nando's, for those who don't know, is a restraunt which specializes flame-grilled peri peri chicken. It has restraunts all over the world but originated in South Africa. There are 1094 Nando's in the world, 339 of them in the UK.

The sunset-burger is made up of :

Two flame-grilled chicken thighs, with melting cheese

Smokey red pepper chutney

And a soft sweet roll.

Sugar

The sunset-burger has 2 and a half cubes worth of sugar for a normal size. This isn't that bad if you consider other things we eat and drink sometimes on a daily basis. A bottle of coke has 10 and a half cubes of sugar.. And as you can see from the picture Ben and Jerry's ice cream has 28 cubes of sugar in it!
