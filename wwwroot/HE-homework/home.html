Customers queue for hours to be the first to try Nando's new burger

The sunset burger is Nando's newest burger. It is replacing the Churrasco thigh burger and though most people are thrilled with the new addition, some people are not. But that doesn't change the fact that hundreds of fans queued up in front of restraunts all across the country to taste it.

Nando's, for those who don't know, is a restraunt which specializes flame-grilled peri peri chicken.
