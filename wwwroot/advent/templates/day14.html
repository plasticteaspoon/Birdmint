On the first day of christmas...

My mum, sister and I often make tablet for christmas so I thought you might be interested in the recipe.

Tablet

Ingredients

  • 2lb granulated sugar
  • 3oz margarine
  • 235ml fresh milk
  • 1 tin sweetened condensed milk

Method

  1. Melt the margarine in a pan.
  2. Add the fresh milk and then the sugar.
  3. When quite melted add the condensed milk.
  4. Boil for 15 minutes, stirring all the time, until it is a rich brown colour and thick.
  5. Take off the heat and beat the air out of it.
  6. Pour into a well buttered tin.
  7. Leave to cool and set.
