On the first day of christmas...

Hello, and welcome to my online advent calendar. Due to popular demand I am rerunning this calendar from last year. I have drawn all the pictures you will see (apart from the one behind the title), either by hand or on the computer using paint.

Along with the current day's picture there will either be a riddle on these pages or a clip or recording of me or my family playing something. Check the calendar each morning to view the new page. The answers to riddles will appear on the same screen the next day so be sure to check it to see if you got it right.
