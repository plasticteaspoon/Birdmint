On the eleventh day of christmas...

This flower is one of the first of my drawings and was what got me into drawing.

It was a card for someone and I thought a flower might be nice so I looked up a tutorial and followed it through.

I started thinking about other things I could draw and looked up them as well. Before long I would draw all sorts of things.

When I got good enough I would copy things in real life without needing step by step instructions.
