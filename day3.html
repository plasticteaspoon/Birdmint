On the third day of christmas...

For anyone who was wondering, the picture on the front of the doors is a drawing of a robot head from the anime "Laputa Castle in the Sky". This is one of my favourite films. Studio Ghibli, the studio which made it, have made many more and I have them all.

All of the faces you will see that I've drawn are in this style. I try to copy faces from anime I've seen or look some up online for me to copy. Quite a few of them I've not copied and have used techniques that I've learnt to create my own faces with their own personality. Some of them I have done on the computer and some I have drawn by hand.
